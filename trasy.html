Safety Tips

  • Always check the weather forecast before leaving
  • Wear full protective gear (helmet, gloves, goggles)
  • Choose trails that match your skill level
  • Riding with a friend is always safer
  • Take care of your bike maintenance before riding

What to Bring

  • Water and energy drinks
  • Map and GPS or offline smartphone
  • Set of tools (spoke wrench, pump, keys)
  • Bandage and first aid kit
  • Flashlight or headlamp (evening)

Check the Weather

  • Rain can make trails dangerous
  • Strong wind makes riding difficult on open areas
  • Low temperature requires additional clothing
  • High humidity affects tire grip
  • Check the forecast before every ride!
